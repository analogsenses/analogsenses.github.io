About

Dec 8th, 2010

Analog Senses is an attempt to provide a human look at the tech world, featuring articles, links and quotes about the Digital Life and other interesting stuff currently happening on planet Earth. It is written and produced by Álvaro Serrano.

You can find out more about me and why I started this site here.

Analog Senses was inspired by the work of some incredibly talented people. Here’s a tribute to them, with my gratitude.

Colophon

The site is hosted by MediaTemple, and powered by the latest version of Wordpress.

Special fonts used throughout the site include Lamplighter Script and Atrament Web. The body font is Proxima Nova, which should improve readability, especially on mobile devices.

Images are usually hosted on Flickr!

On the software front, Analog Senses is written, produced and edited using Transmit, Smultron, Dashcode and the Safari Web Browser on a Mac.

The header features custom artwork by David Lanham.

Contact

Analog Senses doesn’t feature comments, but other forms of reader feedback are encouraged and appreciated. If you’d like to ask me anything, send comments, or make suggestions regarding this site, you can get in touch with me by email:

contact@analogsenses.com

If email is not your thing, you can also follow me on Twitter: @analogsenses

Thank you!
